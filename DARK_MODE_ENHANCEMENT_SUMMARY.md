## ğŸ¨ Dark Mode Design Principles Applied

### **1. Warm Dark Backgrounds**
âœ… Using tinted blacks instead of pure `#000000`
- Rose: `#0F0A0D` (brown tint)
- Ocean: `#020617` (blue tint)
- Forest: `#021208` (green tint)

**Why?** Easier on eyes, reduces eye strain, feels warmer

### **2. Elevated Cards**
âœ… Cards are 10-15% lighter than background
- Creates depth perception
- Separates content clearly
- Professional layering

### **3. High-Contrast Text**
âœ… Text is 90-95% lightness
- Bright enough to read easily
- Not harsh/glaring
- Comfortable for long sessions

### **4. Vibrant Accents**
âœ… Accents are 20-30% lighter in dark mode
- Stand out without being harsh
- Maintain brand colors
- Encourage interaction

### **5. Visible Borders**
âœ… Borders are bright enough to see
- Critical for UI structure
- Defines interactive areas
- Improves usability
